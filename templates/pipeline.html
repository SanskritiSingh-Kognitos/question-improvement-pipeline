Question Improvement Pipeline

A comprehensive workflow for enhancing question quality through template matching and AI polishing

Raw questions from CSV files or user input are processed. Questions may contain various formats, typos, or unclear phrasing.

Questions are matched against predefined templates using flexible regex patterns. The system combines question text and lexical path for better matching.
